About GraphCalc 4.0.1

GraphCalc is an all-in-one enhanced calculator application. It can perform everyday arithmetic, statistical analysis, Booleans, cubes, calculus, decimals, derivatives, etc.

It can even create 2 dimensional and 3 dimensional graphs from the equations.

Features of GraphCalc 4.0.1

Create, display, and modify 2D and 3D equations
Modify and display up to six equations, adjust axes, tick marks and the range and resolution of plotted graphs.
Uses OpenGL to render graphs
Various render modes are available. Graphs can be exported as bitmaps.
Easy to use
There are tooltips for each button.
