About FooBillard 3.0

FooBillard is an OpenGL-based billiards game with realistic physics, AI-player and many gametypes such as pool carambol or snooker.

Features of FooBillard 3.0

  • Wood paneled table with gold covers and gold diamonds.
  • Detail switching of balls according to distance.
  • Zoom in/out - hold right mousebutton.
  • FOV ± - hold right mousebutton + CTRL.
  • Rotate - hold left mousebutton.
  • Play 8ball, 9ball, snooker, carambol.
  • Simple AI-Player.
  • Network play.
