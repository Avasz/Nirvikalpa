About Maxima 5.21.1

Maxima has abilities such as: symbolic integration, 3D plotting, matrix manipulation, simultaneous equations, polynomials, rational functions, integration, Todd-coxeter, graphing, bigfloats and an ODE solver.

From primary school to A'levels and University, Maxima has abilities to help with all your mathematical problems.
