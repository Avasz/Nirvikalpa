About Pengupop 2.2.2

Pengupop is an clone of Frozen-Bubble/Bust a Move with a online multiplayer feature. The purpose of Pengupop is to shoot colored orbs into your playfield, so they form groups of three or more. You win if you manage to remove all orbs. You lose if any orb attaches below the white line. In multiplayer mode, any balls that fall down will reappear on your opponent's playfield as a side-effect (and vice-versa).
