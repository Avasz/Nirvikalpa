About GTD TiddlyWiki Plus 2.1.3

GTD Tiddly Wiki is a Getting Things Done variant of TiddlyWiki, a wiki contained in a single webpage whose content can be easily updated in your web browser.

You can edit entries, or "Tidders", by clicking "edit" or double clicking anywhere on the Tiddler. When you click "done" or press [ctrl+enter] your changes are saved.

Features of GTD TiddlyWiki Plus 2.1.3

  • Exists as a single, portable, cross platform file ; it can be installed to your flash or removable drive so you can take GTD TiddlyWiki Plus wherever you work.
  • GTD TiddlyWiki includes a search form to find all of the content available
  • Based on TiddlyWiki, so the plugins available for TiddlyWiki can be used.

GTD TiddlyWiki Plus Install Notes

  • Install / Download gtd_tiddlywiki_plus.exe to extract the gtd_tiddlywiki.htm file to a folder of your choice.
  • Open gtd_tiddlywiki.htm in your web browser. You should bookmark the page or make gtd_tiddywiki.htm your home page so that you can call the page quickly when using your browser.
