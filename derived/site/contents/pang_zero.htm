About Pang Zero 1.3

Pang Zero is a clone and enhancement of Super Pang, a fast-paced action game that involves popping balloons with a harpoon while avoiding contact with the balloons as they bounce around the screen. Harpooning a large balloon splits the balloon into two, each of which can be split into two more....

Up to 6 persons can play together. Lots of game modes, good soundtrack, special balloon powerups, and different types of balloons make this a fun game!
