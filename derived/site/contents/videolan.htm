About VideoLAN 1.1.2

VLC (VideoLAN Client) is a multimedia player for various audio and video formats: MPEG-1, MPEG-2, MPEG-4, DivX, mp3, ogg, etc. as well as DVDs, VCDs and various streaming protocols.

Features of VideoLAN 1.1.2

Video formats
MPEG-1, MPEG-2, DivX 1/2/3 MPEG-4, DivX 5, XviD, 3ivXD4, OGM, Matroska, Sorenson (SVQ 1/3), DV, Cinepak, H263/H263i, MJPEG A/B, WMV 1/2, WMV 3, Indeo Video v3 (IV32).
Audio Formats
Wav, audio cd, WMA, DTS Wav, Raw DV, Raw AAC, Raw ac3/a52 audio, Raw DTS audio, FLAC audio.
Other Formats
DVD, VCD, AVI, ASF/WMV, Ogg/OGM/Annodex, Matroska, MP4, MOV.
Subtitle formats
MicroDVD, Vobsub, SubRIP, SubViewer (v1 & v2), SSA1-4.
Supported Audio CODECs
MPEG Layer 1 and 2, MP3, AC3, LPCM, AAC, Vorbis, WMA 1/2, ADPCM, DV Audio, FLAC, QDM2/QDMC (QuickTime).
Miscellaneous
Audio Visualization Effects.
ID3 tags.
Skins support.
