About FloboPuyo 0.20

Flobopuyo is a clone of the infamous Puyo Puyo. Playing against a computer or human opponent, your goal is to make groups of four or more coloured fancy smiling bubbles (called "puyos") to make them explode and send bad ghost Puyos to your oponent. You win if your opponent reaches the top of his board with puyos.
