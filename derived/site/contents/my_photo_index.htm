About My Photo Index 1.24.3552

My Photo Index is an photo organiser to organise, view and share your digital photos. You can tag photos with keywords to better find such photos later on. Other useful features include the ability to upload photos to Flickr and Facebook within My Photo Index.

Features of My Photo Index 1.24.3552

  • Supports a variety of image file formats including major RAW formats.
  • Image tagging and searching
  • Share Images - can share images within Flickr, Facebook
  • Encrypt photos - can hide private images
  • flexible printing layouts
