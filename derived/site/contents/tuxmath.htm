About TuxMath 1.7.2

Tux, of Math Command is an educational arcade game (in the tradition of Missle Command) that helps kids practice their math facts. The main goal is to make it effective and fun!

Your mission is to protect your penguin's igloos from the math problem "comets" that drop out of the sky. By typing the correct answer and pressing the Space bar or Enter key, the "comet" is destroyed. Lessons are included from simple number typing through addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of positive and negative numbers.
