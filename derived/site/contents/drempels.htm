About Drempels 1.5.0

Drempels makes colourful, swirling, patterns that can be run as a typical screensaver or can run in your Windows background or "desktop", replacing your old still-image wallpaper with gently-animating visuals. You can still use your computer as you normally would. (Download/view Drempels movie)

Drempels works on Windows 9x, ME, NT, 2000 and XP. It requires DirectX 5.0 or later and an MMX-capable processor.
