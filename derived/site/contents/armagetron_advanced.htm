About Armagetron Advanced 0.2.8.3.1

Armagetron Advanced is a single player as well as online/lan multiplayer arcade style game, where you drive a light cycle. (Based on the movie Tron.) Your light cycle leaves a wall behind you, and you must avoid hitting other players walls, while trying to get as many to hit your wall as possible. Supports up to 16 players
