About Hydrogen 0.9.4-svn

Hydrogen is a pattern-based software synthesizer which emulates an advanced drum machine. Hydrogen makes it easy to create your own drum beats. Be sure to open the demo songs (Ctrl-D) included with Hydrogen to give you an idea of Hydrogen's capabilities.

Features of Hydrogen 0.9.4-svn

  • Sample-based stereo audio engine, with import of sound samples in .wav, .au and .aiff formats.
  • Support of samples in compressed FLAC file.
  • Pattern-based sequencer, with unlimited number of patterns and ability to chain patterns into a song.
  • Up to 64 ticks per pattern with individual level per event and variable pattern length.
  • 64 instrument tracks with volume, mute, solo, pan capabilities.
  • Multi layer support for instruments (up to 16 samples for each instrument).
  • Ability to import/export song files.
  • Unique human velocity, human time, pitch and swing functions.
  • Multiple patterns playing at once.
  • Import/export of drumkits.
  • Export song to wav file or MIDI file.
